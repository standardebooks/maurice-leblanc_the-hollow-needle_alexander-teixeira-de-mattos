Maurice Leblanc.

Alexander Teixeira de Mattos.

Robin Whittleton,

for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans available at the
Internet Archive.

a painting completed in 1899 by
Paul Cézanne.

League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
